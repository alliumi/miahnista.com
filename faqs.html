What is Miahnista?

Miahnista is our official website and digital time capsule for the Jeremiah section of 11-HUMSS DPRO SHS. It serves as a repository of photos, videos, and memorable moments that capture our journey together throughout the school year.

Who are we?

We are Jeremiah—a dynamic, diverse community from Olivarez College Parañaque known for our academic achievements, competitive spirit, and camaraderie. Under the guidance of adviser Vincent Quintero, we celebrate each challenge and victory as part of our unique collective story.

What is the purpose of this website?

The website is designed to preserve our shared experiences and milestones. It not only captures our day-to-day memories but also serves as a tribute to the relationships and adventures that have made our time together unforgettable—ensuring that even as time passes, our special moments remain accessible.

How can Miahnista help us reconnect us with our past?

Miahnista functions as a digital archive that allows current and future members of Jeremiah to revisit precious memories, celebrate our growth, and reflect on the strong bonds formed during our eleventh grade journey. It’s a reminder of how every success and shared laughter has contributed to shaping who we are today.
