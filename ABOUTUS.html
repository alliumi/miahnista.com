ABOUT US

At Olivarez College Paranaque, Jeremiah - fondly known as Miah - is more than just a section of 11-HUMSS DPRO SHS; it is a dynamic community where academic excellence, competition triumphs, and diverse personalities converge. Guided by adviser Vincent Quintero, we celebrate every challenge and victory as part of our unique journey.

Our website, Miahnista, stands as a living time capsule - a digital repository that preserves our cherished memories, milestones, and shared adventures, ensuring that the essence of our experiences endures.

More than a collection of photos and videos, it is a heartfelt tribute to the relationships and moments that have shaped us, continually inspiring us to look back with nostalgia and move forward with strength.
